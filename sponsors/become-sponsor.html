Fast Facts

Date: February 17-18-19, 2017

Location: Florida Technical College, Orlando, Florida

Estimated Attendance: 300 techies with vast interest in Drupal ranging from newbies to seasoned professionals. 

Program Plan:

  • Friday - full-day paid trainings, code sprint
  • Saturday - full-day 5-6 simultaneous tracks of sessions
  • Sunday - half-day 5-6 simultaneous tracks of sessions

Registration fee: $25 (early-bird), $35 (normal)

Total event budget: TBD

The seventh annual Florida DrupalCamp returns to Florida Technical College in Orlando, Florida. Together with our venue partner, Florida Technical College, the 2017 event is building on the huge success of the 2016 event, which drew more than 200 attendees from across Florida and the Southeast US.

Exciting sponsorship opportunities provide a comprehensive range of promotional options for this year’s DrupalCamp. Let us know if you are interested.

Platinum - $3,000

  • Prime logo placement on every page of the Florida DrupalCamp web site
  • Table in registration area
  • 5-minute block during one of the featured sessions to introduce your company
  • 1 sponsor-labeled session during the event to present a Drupal-related topic relating to your company
  • Copy of non-opted-out attendee names, companies and email addresses
  • Three text ad placements in attendee emails sent out prior to the event by FLDC volunteers
  • Naming rights to one of the event rooms
  • Option to place advertising materials in attendee swag bag
  • Exposure on @fldrupalcamp twitter feed
  • Exposure in any press releases
  • Camp admission for 5 people
  • Limited to 1 sponsor

  • Logo placement on every page of the Florida DrupalCamp web site

  • Your company's logo on a slide by itself introduced during one of the featured sessions

  • Copy of non-opted-out attendee names, companies and email addresses
  • Two text ad placements in attendee emails sent out prior to the event by FLDC volunteers
  • Naming rights to one of the event rooms

  • Camp admission for 2 people

Silver - $500

  • Table in registration area

Bronze - $250
